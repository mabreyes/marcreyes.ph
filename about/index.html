Where it all started

I am data-driven.

It all began when I joined

TensorFlow Dev Summit

Extended in Cagayan

de Oro. The rest is history.

I'm Marc and I love data. I'm still a Computer Science student as of present and will be completing my bachelor's degree in March 2019. Before I was introduced to the world of Machine Learning and AI, I was designing websites and user interfaces for school activities and freelance work. Pursuing a career in Data Science was not something I put a lot of thought into in the past, but things turned around when I joined my first TensorFlow Dev Summit Extended in Cagayan de Oro, and well, the rest was history. Since then, I have been speaking at tech conferences and involved myself in community development projects with Machine Learning.
